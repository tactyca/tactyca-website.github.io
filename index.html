Tactycs

We provide an extensive range of advisory services aimed at guiding companies through transformational processes to cultivate a new essence, enabling smooth execution and efficient delivery of products into the market in a scalable and profitable manner.

  • Executives and Founders Coaching

    Supporting founders and executives by imparting insights gained from 16 years of experience in the start-up and scale-up ecosystem through coaching sessions.

  • Strategy Execution

    Implementation of methodologies to align all the departments and be able to deliver smoothly, using state of the art methodologies with big focus on the OKR Framework. Execution challenges often hinder company progress, and our approach aims to address these effectively.

  • Start-up and Scale-up structuring

    Crafting an adaptable organizational structure designed for scalability, accompanied by tailored processes and rituals to ensure optimal alignment. This encompasses strategic staffing and effective organizational change management practices.

  • Product and Project Management

    Establishing optimal strategies for translating client requests and feedback into products that not only fulfill their needs but also align with the company's overarching objectives. This entails implementing efficient business processes for planning, developing, launching, and managing services.

  • Go To Market (GTM)

    Developing and overseeing the implementation of a strategic plan focused on customer engagement and the promotion of products or services, with the ultimate goal of attaining a competitive advantage for the organization.

  • Business Operations

    Development of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) aimed at providing a comprehensive understanding of the company's performance, coupled with the deployment of tools for efficient and streamlined monitoring. These tools are tailored to seamlessly track KPIs across diverse functions, encompassing manufacturing, sales, advertising, and marketing activities.
